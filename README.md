### About me

What started as a high school side hobby — building small web apps for local clinics and shops just to make some pocket money — somehow turned into a full career, and I still enjoy every line of code. Weird how that works out.

These days I mostly live in Vue.js and Nuxt.js, with React showing up whenever a client project asks nicely. I care a lot about clean architecture, fast-loading pages, and code that a teammate can actually read six months later without messaging me at 11pm.

Outside of code, I'm chasing goals two ways: literally, playing football with friends on weekends (and in eFootball, RIP PES 😅), and figuratively, as a husband and proud dad to my daughter Layla, who is a much tougher critic than any client I've had.

- 🔭 Currently exploring my next opportunity — Frontend Developer or Tech Lead role, remote-friendly
- 🌱 Always poking at some new framework or tool, just to see what breaks
- 📍 Based in Sohag, Egypt — open to remote and open to relocating
- 💬 Ask me about Vue, Nuxt, frontend architecture, or why your bundle size is bigger than it should be
